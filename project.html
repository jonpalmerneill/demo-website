ElevenLabs

A next-generation operating system interface that reimagines how people interact with their devices — built for clarity, speed, and expression.

The brief called for a complete redesign of the interface layer — from the home screen to system settings. Working closely with TechCorp's product team, we mapped every existing interaction pattern and rebuilt the visual language from scratch, stripping away years of accumulated complexity to reveal what actually mattered.

The resulting design system pairs a constrained typographic palette with fluid, physics-based motion to create an experience that feels alive. Every animation responds to velocity and intent rather than triggering on a fixed timer — a subtle distinction that users feel before they can articulate it.

Accessibility was embedded from day one. The colour system meets WCAG AA across all surfaces, and a high-contrast mode is built into the core token architecture — not bolted on afterward. The outcome is a platform that serves a global audience without compromise.

"Carnevale didn't just deliver a great interface — they changed how our whole team thinks about what design quality actually means."

— Sarah Chen, VP of Design at TechCorp
